Recently Asked Questions

“How to treat yellow leaves in paddy?”
Ensure proper nitrogen supply and check for pest or waterlogging. Specific fertilizer or pest solution may be advised after diagnosis[web:1].
“Best fertilizer for flowering coconut?”
Apply recommended NPK mix during flowering; supplement with organic manure for better nut set and health[web:1].
“What causes black spots on banana leaves?”
Black Sigatoka fungus or water stress may cause this; use recommended fungicide and improve drainage[web:1].
“How to control coconut palm weevil?”
Sanitation and pheromone traps help prevent infestation. Remove infected tissue, and treat with expert-recommended pesticide[web:1].
